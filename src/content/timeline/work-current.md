<div class="i18n-en">

Interned on TikTok's **Customer Service Platform (CSP)** intelligent-service team, working on one throughline: evaluating chatbot service quality, and answering the meta-question of whether the evaluation itself is accurate.

Led the **New Rule migration** on the Manual Annotation platform: rebuilt the scoring standard from the legacy single-score, CSAT-weakly-correlated GE Rate (PI/RQ/IE) into a two-axis **SQS (Service Quality Score) + UES (User Experience Score)** architecture — adding gating cascades, Knowledge-Source-based scoring branches (Skill / FAQ / SOP), auto-generated Human Result records for bot-to-human handoffs, and an A/B/C double-blind QC workflow; migrated the dashboard's core metrics from GE Rate to SQS/UES averages and QC accuracy.

Also supported the AI evaluator's shift from a fixed workflow to an **Agentic architecture**, building the corresponding MD optimization process to raise evaluation accuracy, and helped align the Session List platform's B/C-side interaction to improve case review and annotation efficiency.
